## üìà Key Themes Across All 80 Videos

### **Recurring Lessons:**

1. **Don't hire broken, bitter, or fractional people**
   - Hire people who want to "play the game 48 minutes"
   - Check if strategist or doer (avoid "strategist" on LinkedIn)

2. **Founders must stay in sales longer than expected**
   - Even with VP Sales, founder = "dedicated middler"
   - Don't step out until VP Sales can demo as well as you

3. **Community is infrastructure, not marketing**
   - Build community into product (like AutoRev's garage + AL)
   - Community drives retention, referrals, and feature feedback

4. **Freemium requires massive scale**
   - Only works with network effects or viral loops
   - Premium-only can be better for AI/SaaS (higher LTV)
   - Hybrid models often win (limited free + paid tiers)

5. **Measure results, not happiness**
   - Customer success = customer achieves outcomes
   - Track what successful customers do, help others replicate
   - NPS is vanity, retention is reality

6. **Don't weaponize customer success**
   - Moving CS into sales creates churn
   - Someone must own "make customers 100% happy"

7. **Marketing budget cuts = cutting your future**
   - Cutting sales = cutting present revenue
   - Cutting marketing = cutting future pipeline
   - "Free" marketing (podcasts, blogs) has high soft costs

8. **Vertical SaaS wins through deep focus**
   - Know your niche better than anyone
   - Community-led GTM beats paid acquisition in verticals
   - Old-school relationship sales still works
