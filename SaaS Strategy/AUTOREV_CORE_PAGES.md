# AutoRev Core Pages — Purpose & Description

---

## My Garage

**Purpose:** Your vehicle hub — add cars, plan builds, visualize upgrades, find parts.

### What It Does

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Add Vehicles** | Add any car from our database to your personal garage |
| **Vehicle Specs** | See complete specifications for your car (power, torque, weight, dimensions, etc.) |
| **Build & Upgrade** | Select modifications and upgrades for your vehicle |
| **Performance Impact** | Visualize what each mod does to your car's performance before you commit |
| **Parts Navigation** | See the specific parts you need for each upgrade — with fitment verification and value positioning |
| **Photos** | View and manage photos of your build |

### User Journey

```
Add Car → View Specs → Select Upgrades → See Performance Impact → Find Parts → Build with Confidence
```

### Problems Solved

- **Problem 1:** "What should I do to my car?" — Objective-driven upgrade paths
- **Problem 2:** "Which parts should I buy?" — Fitment-verified, value-positioned parts

### The Pitch

> *"My Garage is your build command center. Add your car, explore upgrades, see exactly what they'll do for performance, and find the right parts — all in one place. No more forum rabbit holes. No more guessing."*

## My Data

**Purpose:** Performance validation — see what you should get, log what you actually get, know what's next.

### What It Does

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Dyno Estimator** | See estimated dyno results (HP/torque curves) based on your car's modifications |
| **Dyno Data Upload** | Upload your actual dyno results to compare estimated vs. real |
| **Track Time Estimator** | Get lap time estimates for your car on specific tracks, adjusted for your skill level |
| **Track Data Logging** | Log your actual lap times to track progression |
| **Performance Analysis** | Compare expected vs. actual, identify gaps, get recommendations |

### User Journey

```
See Estimated Performance → Do the Mods → Log Real Data → Compare → Get Next-Step Recommendations
```

### Problems Solved

- **Problem 3:** "What will this actually do for me?" — Translates mods to tangible outcomes
- **Problem 4:** "I did the mods — now what?" — Feedback loop for progression

### The Pitch

> *"My Data answers the question every enthusiast asks: 'Was it worth it?' See what your mods should deliver, log what you're actually getting, and get personalized guidance on what to do next. It's the feedback loop the car community has never had."*

## AL (AI Assistant)

**Purpose:** Instant automotive expertise — your questions answered, no searching, no trolls.

### What It Does

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Conversational AI** | Ask anything about cars, mods, parts, performance — get real answers |
| **Connected to Your Data** | AL knows your garage, your mods, your goals — answers are personalized |
| **AutoRev Database Access** | Taps into our entire parts catalog, vehicle specs, and modification data |
| **Cited Answers** | Provides sources and confidence levels — never bullshits you |

### What Makes AL Different

- **Not generic ChatGPT** — trained and focused specifically on automotive performance
- **Knows YOUR cars** — personalized to your garage and build goals
- **Has OUR data** — access to fitment, parts, specs, community insights
- **Hedges when uncertain** — won't hallucinate; tells you when to verify

### Problems Solved

- **Problem 6:** "I just have a quick question" — Instant expertise, no searching

### The Pitch

> *"AL is the automotive expert you wish you had on speed dial. Ask anything — 'Do I need a tune after headers?' 'What's the best bang-for-buck exhaust for my M3?' 'Why is my car pulling left after coilovers?' — and get accurate, cited answers in seconds. No forum searches. No trolls. Just expertise on tap."*
