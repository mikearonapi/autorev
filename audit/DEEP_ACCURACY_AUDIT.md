## Methodology

For each vehicle, we verify against:
1. **Primary**: Official manufacturer specifications
2. **Secondary**: Car and Driver, MotorTrend, Edmunds instrumented tests
3. **Tertiary**: Wikipedia (for cross-reference)

Key specs verified:
- Horsepower (crank)
- Torque (lb-ft)
- Curb Weight (lbs)
- 0-60 mph time (seconds)
- Drivetrain
