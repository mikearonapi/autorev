# 100 Most Popular Sports Sedans for Automotive Tuning

The tuner sedan market spans **five distinct ecosystems**—JDM, German, American, Korean, and European boutique—each with dedicated aftermarket infrastructure, forums, and ECU tuning solutions. This database covers 1990-present vehicles with verified active modification communities, prioritized by parts availability (Hondata, COBB, APR, Dinan), forum activity, and proven build potential.
