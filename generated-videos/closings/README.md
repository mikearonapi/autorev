# Closing Videos

Place your closing sequences here. Each closing video should be:
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
- **Resolution:** 720x1280 (9:16 vertical)
- **Duration:** 4-5 seconds
- **Audio:** Your branded audio/music

## Required Files

| Persona | Filename | Description |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| Buyers | `CLOSING-BUYERS.mp4` | "Find your perfect car at autorev.app" |
| Owners | `CLOSING-OWNERS.mp4` | "Track your car at autorev.app" |
| Builders | `CLOSING-BUILDERS.mp4` | "Plan your build at autorev.app" |
| Cinematic | `CLOSING-CINEMATIC.mp4` | Generic brand closer |

## Creating Closings

You can create these with:
1. **Canva** - Use their video templates
2. **CapCut** - Add logo animation + text
3. **After Effects** - Full custom motion graphics

Each should include:
- AutoRev logo animation
- CTA text for that persona
- Branded music/sound

The daily generator will automatically append the appropriate closing to each reel.
