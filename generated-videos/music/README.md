# AutoRev Music Library

## Quick Start

1. **Download tracks from Artlist** (or other source)
2. **Drop ALL tracks into the `inbox/` folder**
3. **Ask me to categorize them** - I'll listen and sort into the right folders

## Folder Structure

```
music/
├── inbox/          ← Drop new downloads here
├── buyers/         ← Uplifting, hopeful, inspiring
├── owners/         ← Warm, nostalgic, chill
├── builders/       ← Energetic, powerful, driving
└── cinematic/      ← Epic, emotional, sweeping
```

## Recommended Downloads from Artlist

### For Buyers (Discovery/Aspiration)
Search: "uplifting cinematic", "hopeful inspiring", "motivational"
- Look for: Piano melodies, gentle builds, optimistic feel
- Avoid: Vocals, heavy bass

### For Owners (Pride/Satisfaction)  
Search: "warm acoustic", "nostalgic", "chill driving"
- Look for: Acoustic guitar, mellow vibes, satisfaction
- Avoid: Too energetic, harsh sounds

### For Builders (Power/Achievement)
Search: "energetic electronic", "powerful", "intense cinematic"
- Look for: Driving beats, builds, powerful drops
- Avoid: Too aggressive, distracting vocals

### For Cinematic (Epic/Emotional)
Search: "epic orchestral", "emotional piano", "sweeping cinematic"
- Look for: Orchestral swells, emotional builds, grandeur
- Avoid: Cheesy, overused trailer music

## File Format

- **Format:** MP3 or WAV (MP3 preferred for size)
- **Length:** 30-90 seconds ideal (will be trimmed to 18s)
- **Naming:** Keep original names, I'll rename after categorizing

## Tips

1. Download both "full" and "30s" versions when available
2. Preview in context of car videos before downloading
3. 3-5 tracks per category gives good variety
4. Instrumental only - no vocals
